Predict your stroke risk

Welcome to this site created using machine learning to predict whether you are at risk of a stroke. Using thousands of points of data and a logistic regresion machine learning model which we found to have a 94% accuracy rate, we are able to predict whether you are at risk of a stroke based on your gender, age, hypertension, BMI and other inputs.
