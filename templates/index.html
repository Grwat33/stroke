Stroke Predictive Model



Welcome to this site created using machine learning to predict whether you are at risk of a stroke. Using thousands of points of data and a logistic regression model which we found to have a 94% accuracy rate, we are able to predict whether you are at risk of a stroke based on your gender, age, hypertension, BMI and other inputs.

Disclaimer: This site is for demonstration purposes of machine learning only and should not be used as a basis for whether you are as risk of stroke. If you believe that you that you might be as risk, please reach out to your doctor for professional advice.
